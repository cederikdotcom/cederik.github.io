Workshop Options Overview

Inspirational Talk

Description: This session serves as an introduction to The Nims Framework, focusing on its conceptual foundation and the philosophy behind it. The talk is designed to inspire participants by illustrating the innovative thinking that drives the framework.

  • Duration: 30 Minutes
  • Content Includes:
    • Introduction to the concepts and values of The Nims Framework.
    • Discussion on the evolving landscape of technology and its impact on businesses and individuals.
    • Cross-industry Inspirational examples demonstrating the power of forward-thinking approaches.

General Introduction Workshop

Description: This workshop includes the Inspirational Talk plus a brief brainstorming session tailored to the participants' organization, providing an overview and initial application ideas.

  • Duration: 1.5 hours
  • Content Includes:
    • Everything covered in the Inspirational Talk.
    • A 30-minute brainstorming session focusing on potential areas for XR integration within the organization.
    • A 15-minute idea pitching moment by all participants.
    • Brief discussions on quick-win strategies for immediate implementation.

Industry Standard Workshop

Description: This workshop provides a deeper dive into The Nims Framework with practical examples from various organizations, offering actionable insights tailored to participants' industries.

  • Duration: 2 hours
  • Content Includes:
    • Interactive presentation detailing The Nims Framework with real-world examples.
    • Q&A session for discussing applications and clarifying concepts.
    • Examples relevant to the participants' industry or sector, demonstrating how the framework can address specific issues.

The Challenge

As humans, we double our collective knowledge approximately every 13 months, and turn it into technology at an even faster pace.

This accelerating growth creates a widening gap between:

  • General Managers and Their Specialized Workforce: Managers struggle to keep pace with the specialized knowledge of their teams, leading to communication and alignment challenges.
  • Parents and Their Children: The generational divide grows as parents and children face increasingly different experiences and knowledge bases.
  • Individuals with Different Backgrounds: The diversity of backgrounds and expertise makes effective communication and understanding more challenging.
  • You and the World: The rapid pace of change makes it harder to stay connected with global developments and trends, leading to a growing sense of not being able to follow.
